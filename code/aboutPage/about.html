Our Team

We are a team of volunteers who created the PurrfectHome project to help homeless animals find their home and new family! Often in cities, when they are found on the streets, they are taken to a shelter for homeless animals. But not all shelters have the ability to contain a large number of cats and dogs for a long time, therefore, in the end, they are euthanized. We want our little friends to have the same right to a long life in warmth and love in the family. We gathered volunteers in different cities around the world and contacted all possible shelters to help every little cat, dog or other species, to give them a new life.

Let's try together! You can be the one to take one of this little happiness home and give one more reason for the smile of your family. Every day we make posts in our News tab (which is available after registering and logging in your account), in which we write about animals that appeared in the shelters. If you are interested, then follow the special form, and we will contact you by e-mail.We can also offer you to join our friendly team and become one of the volunteers. You can invest in a project and help us.

We will be glad to see you in our project!
